Young Dance

Transforming Lives Through Movement
Young Dance is an inclusive, artistic community where creativity flourishes and all are encouraged to aspire to artistic professionalism. Young Dance offers modern dance technique, improvisation, and choreographic skills for any body.

Music Play with Sarah

Early Childhood Music Classes for children ages 0-3
These classes will immerse young children in a rich musical environment and provide a variety of experiences designed to expand musical understanding and skills in an active and playful way. Class activities include singing and chanting, moving to music (including use of props such as bean bags, scarves and streamers), playing assorted small percussion instruments, pattern instruction, and imaginative musical play. The goal of this program is to increase children’s musical aptitude (potential to achieve in music) through engaging and entertaining musical activities, which will lay a foundation for lifelong music making and enjoyment.

Improv Parenting

Improv Parenting is offering a cool improv class at Fridays 4:30-5:30 p.m. starting September 23rd. Improv Parenting classes are fun, active, and educational, without pressure to perform – we apply improv skills to real life!

Not-yet-a-young-adult and no-longer-a-child, 11-14 year-olds’ lives are full of transitions. Between the expectations in school and evolving relationships at home, this age group needs places to assert their voice without judgment. And they need permission to keep playing like a kid. We offer a playful, fun space to explore the pushing and pulling of home, school and a more complex social world.
