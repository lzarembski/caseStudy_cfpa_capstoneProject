The following practitioners offer private sessions or group classes aimed at considering the integration of body and mind. Please contact the instructors directly for further information and registration.

Twin Cities Music Therapy Services, LLC

Twin Cities Music Therapy Services, LLC. is dedicated to providing the highest quality services using music to promote health through empowering children and adults of all abilities to become actively invested in their process of growth. Music therapy sessions are music-centered and creatively facilitated through individualized, evidence-based treatment.

Twin Cities Music Therapy Services, LLC provides music therapy services to people of all ages and abilities, promoting goal directed development. We specialize in working with children and adults with autism, developmental, physical, and neurologic disabilities.

Taking Root Massage Therapy

Taking Root Massage Therapy utilizes both deep tissue work (rehabilitative massage and trigger point therapy) along with relaxation massage techniques. I have also studied Myofascial Release with John Barnes and am certified in Bodywork for the Childbearing Year, a program designed by expert pregnancy bodyworker, Kate Jordan. Before the start of each there is an intake conversation where we discuss your goals for the session along with any physical concerns you may have. Each session is unique and adapted to address your individual goals and concerns. With every session it is my intention to provide support that increases your wellness, aids your healing process, and leads to growth.

Deep Peace Bodyworks Massage Studio

There is no “standard massage” at Deep Peace Bodyworks. Deep Peace Bodyworks always customize your massage based on what you request, your health history, and what I find in your tissue once I get started. Some clients want a relaxing full-body massage, some want extra attention to one or several areas, and some want very focused work on particular sore muscles, knots, or old injuries. I am grateful for this variety in my practice. Deep Peace Bodyworks look forward to finding out just what sort of massage you want, and collaborating with you to provide a deeply satisfying massage experience.

Heaton-Hill Healing Touch

Healing Touch is a relaxing, nurturing, energy therapy. Gentle touch assists in balancing your physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being. Healing Touch works with your energy field to support your natural abilities to balance and heal: Reduces stress, provides relaxation, creates a sense of well-being, calms anxiety, relieves depression, decreases and helps manage pain, strengthens the immune system, enhances recovery from surgery, reduces the length of hospital stays, complements care for neck and back problems, reduces the effects of trauma, deepens spiritual connection, enhances personal and spiritual growth, supports cancer care, provides support during radiation and chemotherapy, eases acute and chronic conditions, supports the dying process

Healing Touch is accepted, validated, and utilized in hospitals, hospice, long-term care, integrative health centers, private practices, and clinics.
